About Me

Engineer by passion,
builder by nature.

Hey there! My name is Krish — an undergraduate Electrical Engineering student at the University of Texas at Dallas. My fascination with engineering started young: building Lego structures and designing Hot Wheels tracks sparked the curiosity that now drives me to understand how complex systems work and how smart design makes them faster and more reliable.

Through coursework and hands-on involvement with Dallas Formula Racing EV and IEEE, I've built strong skills in circuit debugging, prototyping, schematic design, and industry-standard tools including oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, and PCB layout software.

My key interests include mixed-signal & RF circuit design, systems engineering, and telecommunications systems. Currently seeking a summer internship or grad school opportunity to contribute to meaningful projects alongside mentors who value innovation.
